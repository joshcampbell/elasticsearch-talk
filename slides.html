Elasticsearch

So Many Joins

  • A document is not a document.
  • Flexibility + denormalization = tons of joins
  • Searching and reporting are complex and expensive

So Do Them Ahead of Time

  • Secondary reporting database
  • NoSQL / document stores
  • Cache the results of expensive operations

Two Weeks' Research

  • Cassandra - kind of awful
  • Redis - Great, but not what we need
  • Elasticsearch - very promising

What is Elasticsearch?

  • Data distributed redundantly across cluster of servers
  • Documents indexed by Lucene (venerable search library)
  • Documents subdivided into typed fields
  • Communicates using JSON over HTTP
